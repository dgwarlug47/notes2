## Bridge 1
Like Brian Jones  
I was born to swim towards a month ago
- Reference to Brian Jones' death by drowning - he died approximately one month after being fired from The Rolling Stones.
> **[MYTAKE]** The narrator identifies with Brian Jones' tragic trajectory toward death - a symbol of musical talent destroyed by drugs and existential drift. Winter interprets Jones' drowning as a desperate attempt to reconnect with The Rolling Stones after being fired due to substance abuse. His death was ruled "death by misadventure," but here represents the artist's self-destructive pursuit of meaning through music.

Towards the Rolling Stones  
Towards me and you  
Swimming alone
